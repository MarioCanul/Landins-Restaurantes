Fine Seafood & Steaks in a spectacular waterfront setting

Choose Captain’s Cove. Nestled on the shores of Nichupte Lagoon at the heart of the Cancun Hotel Zone, Captain’s Cove steakhouse and seafood grill is waterfront dining at its best. Offering a tempting menu of fresh seafood and prime steaks, it has been captivating visitors and locals for 35 years, a true Cancun classic. Our thatched Caribbean-style restaurant offers a choice of beautiful settings for memorable dining moments.
